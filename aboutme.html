My name is Kris Nguyen, and I am a web developer. With an inherent love of writing code to solve intricate issues, I have been programming since age 16. I taught myself how to code through reading books and watching online tutorials. My love for developing innovative and creative websites for clients is what has opened opportunities for me to work in-house and remotely on projects for startups, agencies, leading brands, and charities. I care deeply about creating top-notch, useful and appealing sites that meet the needs of clients and that help people to make a difference. I believe that there is more room for me to develop professionally, and this is what prompted me to join this company. My love for web developing and dedication to my profession will make me an effective member of your team. I look forward to working with you.
